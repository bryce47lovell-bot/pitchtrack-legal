Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

PitchTrack is developed by Bryce Lovell ("I," "me," or "my"). This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and your rights as a user.

By using PitchTrack — including through Apple TestFlight — you agree to this policy.

Data I Collect

Data you provide:

  • Account information (email address, display name) when you sign up
  • Pitch session data you log — velocity, pitch type, session notes, date and time

Data collected automatically:

  • Basic usage analytics (which features are used, app crashes)
  • Device type and OS version for debugging purposes

I do not collect your location, contacts, or any data unrelated to pitching sessions.

How I Use Your Data

  • To save and display your pitch history and stats
  • To generate your shareable session cards
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns
  • To send transactional emails such as account confirmation — no marketing emails without your consent

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. Firebase is subject to Google's own privacy standards. Data is stored on servers in the United States.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Firebase / Google — cloud storage and authentication
  • RevenueCat — managing Pro subscriptions (once payments are live)
  • If required by law

TestFlight Beta

During the beta period via Apple TestFlight, crash logs and feedback may be collected by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles that data.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics (coming in a future update)

To make any of these requests, contact me at brycelovell.dev@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

PitchTrack is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact me and I'll delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy as the app grows. If there's a significant change, I'll notify users through the app or via email.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at brycelovell.dev@gmail.com.
